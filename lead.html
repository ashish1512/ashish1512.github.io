Leadership Roles & Activities

President

Mar 2014 – Oct 2015
IEEE Student Branch Manipal, MIT-Manipal

As the Chairperson of IEEE Student Branch Manipal, I lead and drove the team towards achieving IEEE's broader aim of empowering the world through technology and fostering technological innovation. We, as a part of IEEE, organized 16 events across computer science, electronics and mechanical streams during the annual IEEE technical fest, Techweekend.
